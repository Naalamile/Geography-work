ABOUT US

Learn about what we do

Welcome to HeritageQuest, a pioneering open-access platform dedicated to the management and dissemination of archaeological data and information in Ghana. Our mission is to make archaeological information more accessible to both academic and non-academic audiences, fostering a deeper understanding of Ghana’s rich cultural heritage.

OUR VISION

We envision a world where archaeological knowledge transcends borders and can be shared freely. Our platform addresses the needs of different audiences by offering content that is both academically thorough and publicly accessible. Through blog posts, summaries, and detailed reports, we cater to the general public while ensuring that in-depth research remains accessible to scholars and archaeologists.

Why We Exist

Our project is rooted in the belief that archaeology plays a crucial role in preserving and interpreting the past, not just for academic circles but for the public as well. We are dedicated to developing a relational database system that centralizes archaeological data from various sources, including excavations, fieldwork, and research projects conducted across Ghana. With decades of archaeological practice behind us, we aim to curate and archive data in a way that promotes easy access, management, and efficient retrieval.

Our Features

Data Centralization: The platform curates digitized primary data, reducing the physical storage burden and improving collaboration between researchers.

Public Engagement: We simplify complex academic reports into engaging blog posts for the general public, making archaeology approachable and interesting for everyone.

Join us as we bring the story of Ghana’s past into the future.
